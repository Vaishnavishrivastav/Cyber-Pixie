What we make

A video model that actually speaks anime.

General-purpose video generators optimize for photorealism first. We're building the opposite: a model and toolset specialized for anime motion, line quality, and camera language from the ground up.

one

Motion, not just frames

Fight choreography, hair and cloth physics, speed lines, impact frames — trained on the motion language anime actually uses, not generic interpolation.

two

Character lock

Keep a character consistent across shots and scenes — same design, same energy — without redrawing your reference every time.

three

Director-grade controls

Camera moves, transformation sequences, and VFX presets described in the vocabulary storyboard artists already use.

How it works

From idea to shot, in four steps.

01 — input

Describe the shot

Prompt, reference image, or a rough sketch. Whatever you have is enough to start.

02 — direction

Set the camera

Pick motion, pacing, and framing in plain, film-set language.

03 — generate

Render the clip

Our anime-specialized model handles motion, line quality, and consistency.

04 — refine

Lock the cut

Adjust, regenerate, or extend the shot until it's the one you want.
